# Style Learning

Use style as guidance, not handcuffs.

## Goal

Capture the feel of reference prose without copying sentences or destroying narrative clarity.

## Extract two layers

### 1. Qualitative guide (`style_guide.md`)
Record:
- sentence energy
- paragraph rhythm
- narrative distance
- diction level
- sensory bias
- humor/irony/gravity level
- dialogue density
- taboo moves

### 2. Quantitative profile (`style_profile.json`)
Optionally record:
- average sentence length
- paragraph length range
- dialogue ratio
- lexical diversity estimate
- repeated rhetorical patterns
- favorite transition words

## Application rules

- keep plot clarity above style mimicry
- do not imitate surface tics so hard that every paragraph looks patterned
- avoid importing another author's signature metaphors verbatim
- if style conflicts with character clarity, choose clarity

## Audit questions

- does the style overlay blur who knows what?
- does imitation create repetitive sentence cadence?
- does the chapter sound borrowed rather than alive?
